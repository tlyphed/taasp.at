On Tuesday morning (9:00 - 12:30) the workshop is held in [HS 1](https://maps.tuwien.ac.at/?q=QA0101) on the first floor of the Theresianium building at [Theresianumgasse 27, 1040 Wien](https://maps.app.goo.gl/UVGd8oqdZMV25SPq6).

On Tuesday afternoon the workshop is held in [Seminarraum 363](https://maps.tuwien.ac.at/?q=CA0138) on the first floor of the EI building at [Gußhausstraße 27-29, 1040 Wien](https://maps.app.goo.gl/Nfp2Bd5fefckWyFd9).
